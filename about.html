✦ Beyond the Page ✦
Creative Writing & Roleplay Club
School Club · Founded Sophomore Year · Mentor: Michael

Club session, Providence High School

What started as twelve people in a room with a shared love of stories has become something stranger and better. The club runs like a writing workshop crossed with a game session — each meeting begins with a prompt, ends with something none of us expected.

By junior year, thirty members. Mentor: Michael. We've produced stories, characters, worlds. A few of them are in this portfolio.

✦ Background ✦
Where I'm From

Los Angeles · Rio de Janeiro · Italy · UK. Growing up between languages and cultures is the best possible education for a writer — you learn early that there is never only one way to say something.

What I Make

Stories, scripts, drawings, films, games. Everything feeds everything else. A game teaches you about structure. Drawing teaches you to see. Film teaches you timing. Writing is where it all ends up.
